Top 3 popular K-drama series Accoding to Amany Rady

My top 3 K-drama series of all the time

Crash Landing on You

A romantic drama about a South Korean heiress who accidentally lands in North Korea and falls in love with a North Korean army officer.

Itaewon Class

A drama about an ex-convict who opens a bar in the trendy neighborhood of Itaewon to seek revenge against a powerful food corporation.

Goblin

A fantasy romance drama about a goblin who has lived for 900 years and is searching for his bride to end his immortal life.
